Port Townsend Fort Warden Family Reunion

During the early summer of 2022, All of my thirty cousins, seven aunts and uncles, and grandparents all went up to a small fort on a peninsula located in Washington. At the end of this exciting event, one of my family closest family members joined us for a few last adventures before flying home.

Another place we went was to a place I've gone my entire childhood; The Reptile Zoo. The other family and I share a love of animals, so it was the perfect place. We did a scavenger hunt, held some snakes, petted some turtles, and had a female lunch on the tables right outside the zoo, next to a life-sized statue of a Crocodile. At the edge of the property, several large blackberry bushes were growing wild, so we had a few of those with our lunch too. This vacation was one of my favorites because I loved the time I had with my family in a familiar place.

Xander, Natalie, Michelle, Ada, Rachel, me, Amber, Nessa, Candace
